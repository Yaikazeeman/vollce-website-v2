Solution

This is Vollce™ V1, by far the most sustainable solution for mobility in our fastly changing society. the Vollce™ V1 is developed following high Dutch quality standards.

This ensures a state of the art and durable product. Nevertheless, our V1 is the most affordable product in the current market. We enable future customers to choose a serious sustainable alternative

Why?

The use of a V1 is more cost efficient than a petrol engine. Avoid high maintenance costs or expensive fuel prices. Earn your investment back within a few years and keep driving at low cost.

The Vollce V1™ helps reducing the ecological footprint current scooters are responsible for. By taking responsibility and driving a more ecological solution people can still drive their scooter but in a clean matter.

Due to environmental requirements a lot of scooters are not allowed in the city of Amsterdam and Utrecht. These requirements are becoming more strict and more important to other municipalities. Enjoy the freedom of driving your scooter without having to think about the environment or other expensive alternatives.

The Vollce™ V1 is completely recyclable and sustainable. No need to sell your petrol scooter in order to get an electric one. Vollce believes in an affordable, sustainable and easy implementable solution. Besides, the V1 has by far the smallest footprint in the entire electromobility industry.

About us

Vollce™ is an innovative electric mobility company based in Amsterdam. It is our ambition to create resourceful products that reinforce the current market. Our first product will be introduced in Q1 2020. Founded in 2019, based in Amsterdam.
