Solution

This is Vollce V1, by far the most sustainable solution for mobility in our fastly changing society. Vollce V1 is developed following high Dutch quality standards.

This ensures a state of the art and durable product. Nevertheless, V1 is the most affordable product in the current market. We enable future customers to choose a serious sustainable alternative.

Why?

The use of V1 is more cost efficient than a petrol engine. Avoid high maintenance costs or expensive fuel prices. Earn your investment back within a few years and keep driving at low cost.

Vollce V1 helps reducing the ecological footprint current scooters are responsible for. By taking responsibility and driving a more ecological solution people can still drive their scooter but in a clean matter.

Due to environmental requirements a lot of scooters are not allowed in the city of Amsterdam and Utrecht. These requirements are becoming more strict and more important to other municipalities.

Vollce V1 is 98.2% recyclable. No need to sell your petrol scooter in order to get an electric one. Vollce believes in an affordable, sustainable and easy-to-use solution.

“Besides, V1 has by far the smallest footprint in the entire electromobility industry”

About us

Vollce is an innovative electric mobility company based in Amsterdam. It is our ambition to create resourceful products that reinforce the current market. Our first product will be introduced in Q1 2020.
